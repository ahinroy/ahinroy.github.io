Thanks for stopping by my research page! Currently I am working as a Research Fellow at Advanced Microscopy Laboratory, Trinity College Dublin. My current research involves atomic-scale scanning transmission electron microscopy (STEM) and density functional theory (DFT) based simulations of functional nanomaterials, along with their synthesis. A primary focus of such a combinatorial approach is to establish structure-property relationship in nanomaterials for targeted applications. Topical interest lies in wet-chemical synthesis, quantitative electron microscopy and simulations of inorganic nanomaterials for catalysis and electronic devices.
