Below are a few pointers for different plausible roles you can take up in the group.

Postdoctoral Research Associate


If you are a fresh graduate or a motivated early-career researcher in nanomaterials, you can join the group in extramurally funded projects. Watch this space for such vacancies.

Also, there are several national level postdoctoral fellowships offered by Government of India & also institutional fellowship by IIT-KGP. If you are interested, please contact Ahin to discuss the project in advance and draft the necessary research proposal. Some such external schemes are (i) SERB National Postdoctoral Fellowship scheme, (ii) UGC Postdoctoral Fellowship for Women scheme, (iii) DST Women Scientist scheme, (iv) UGC D.S. Kothari Postdoctoral Fellowship scheme and (v) IIT-KGP Postdoctoral Fellowship.

Prospective Graduate (Ph. D) Students


We are constantly seeking to have motivated graduate students, who are interested to pursue PhD thesis work on structure-property ralationship in inorganic functional nanomaterials. Please have a look at the IIT-KGP official PhD program. Once you are qualified for such a programme, you are most welcome to discuss your Ph. D. project work with Ahin.

Bachelors'/Masters' Project Students


IIT Kharagpur has internationally renowned undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in different areas. If you are a student here and is interested in our research and want to pursue your undergraduate or postgraduate thesis, please contact Ahin directly for possible directions. We are looking for folks with background in Chemistry/ Materials Science/ Physics. Also, if you are in any stream of engineering with a bit of experience in coding (python preferable), give us a shout for a stint in our group, if you fancy tinkering with real-world electron microscopy problems!
