We are building up the wet-chemical lab facilities for synthesis of nanomaterials. As we are a group focusing a lot on the structure-property correlation, we have a suite of instruments to study catalysis in two ways, viz. thermal catalysis in gas-phase and electrochemical catalysis. We study reactions important for energy generation and storage.
